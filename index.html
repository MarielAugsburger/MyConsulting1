About Me

Mariel Augsburger is currently a studio art and emerging technology in business design double major at Miami University in Oxford, Ohio. She enjoys running long distances and birdwatching in the woods. Mariel's favorite art medium to work with is handbuilding clay sculptures. Born May 16, 2005, she has two younger brothers named Christian and Seth. She has two dogs, a golden retriever named Beans and a havanese named Jasper, and an incredibly old and fat shubunkin goldfish named Chubbo. Her hometown is Wapakoneta, a rural town in the northeast area of Ohio. Her favorite song is "Perfect" by The Smashing Pumpkins, her favorite album is Jar of Flies by Alice in Chains, and her favorite band is Alice in Chains. Her favorite movies are Megamind, Rango, and Spirited Away. Mariel's dream job would be to work at Laika Studios in Hillsboro, Oregon, which would allow her to be a part of creating one-of-a-kind stop motion animated movies.
